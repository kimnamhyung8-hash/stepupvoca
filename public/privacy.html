Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

VocaQuest (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • Account Data: Name, email, and profile picture provided via Google Login.
  • Usage Data: Learning progress, quiz results, battle stats, and owned Points.
  • Device Information: Ad ID (IDFA/AAID), device model, OS version.
  • AI Input: Text and voice data sent for translation or conversation (processed via Google Gemini API).

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners to provide our services:

  • Google Firebase: Account authentication and database management.
  • Google Generative AI (Gemini): Artificial intelligence translation and text analysis.
  • Google AdMob: Provides in-app ads.

3. Safety & User Generated Content (UGC) Policy

To ensure a safe environment in services like Global Live Chat:

  • We monitor for harmful or inappropriate content.
  • Users can report violations through the reporting tool.
  • Violating users may be restricted or banned from the service.

4. Data Security and Retention Period

User account information and learning data are securely stored until the account is deleted. Upon request for account deletion, the collected information is immediately destroyed.

5. Protection of Children's Personal Information

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will take immediate action to delete it.
